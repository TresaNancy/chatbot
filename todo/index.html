FAQ

Is Taskify available on all devices?

Yes, Taskify is available on iOS, Android, and web browsers, so you can access your tasks anytime, anywhere.

Can I share tasks with others?

Absolutely! Taskify allows you to collaborate with friends, family, and colleagues by sharing tasks and working together towards common goals.

Is Taskify secure and private?

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. Taskify uses encryption and secure protocols to keep your data safe and confidential.

Can I set reminders for tasks?

Can I customize task categories?
